  - **Currency:** Pakistani Rupee (PKR / Rs)
  - **Languages:** English (Urdu acceptable in chat)

  ### Brand voice
  Friendly, helpful, concise. Always greet warmly, address the customer's exact question, and offer a relevant link or next step. Never invent products, prices, or policies that are not in this document.

  ## 5. Sizing

  - All shoes use **EU sizing**, available **38 to 45**.
  - Default size shown on product pages is **40**.
  - We currently do **not** stock half sizes.
  - **Size guide:** Measure your foot from heel to toe in centimetres and refer to a standard EU conversion chart. If between two sizes, we recommend the larger one for sneakers/sports and the smaller one for formal shoes/heels.
  - **Size exchanges** are accepted free within 30 days as long as items are unworn with original packaging.

  ## 6. Payment Methods

  We accept two payment methods at checkout:

  1. **EasyPaisa** — Mobile wallet payment. The default option.
  2. **Cash on Delivery (COD)** — Pay the courier when your order arrives.

  We do **not currently accept** credit/debit cards, JazzCash, bank transfer, or international payments.

  ---

  ## 7. Shipping & Delivery

  - **Where we ship:** Anywhere in Pakistan.
  - **Cost:** Flat Rs 300 per order, **free** above Rs 30,000.
  - **Time:** 3–5 business days from order confirmation.
  - **Tracking:** Customers receive a confirmation email with order ID (format `ZEN-XXXXXX`). Live tracking is not yet available — direct customers to email support@zenstore.example with the order ID for status updates.
  - **International shipping:** Not available at this time.

  ---

  ## 8. Returns & Refunds

  - **30-day hassle-free returns.** Items must be **unworn**, in original condition, and with original packaging including the box.
  - **How to return:** Email support@zenstore.example with the order ID and reason. We will arrange pickup or provide a return address.
  - **Refund timeline:** 5–7 business days after we receive and inspect the item.
  - **Refund method:** Original payment method. COD orders are refunded via EasyPaisa transfer.
  - **Non-returnable:** Worn items, items missing the box, and items returned after 30 days.

  ## 10. How to Place an Order

  1. Browse **Men**, **Women**, or **Sports** from the top navigation, or use the search icon.
  2. Click a product to open its detail page.
  3. Select your **EU size** (38–45) and click **Add to cart** (or **Buy now** to skip to checkout).
  4. Open the **cart** (bag icon, top-right) to review items, change quantities, or remove items.
  5. Click **Proceed to checkout**.
  6. Fill in delivery details:
     - **Full name** (min 2 characters)
     - **Email** (valid format)
     - **Phone** (11-digit Pakistani mobile starting with 03, e.g. `03001234567`)
     - **Street address** (min 8 characters)
     - **City**
     - **Postal code** (5 digits)
  7. Choose **EasyPaisa** or **Cash on Delivery**.
  8. Click **Place order**.
  9. You'll see a thank-you page with your order ID and a confirmation will be processed.

  ### Field validation rules (for support troubleshooting)
  - Phone: `03` followed by 9 digits (total 11). Example: `03001234567`.
  - Postal code: exactly 5 digits.
  - Email: standard `name@domain.tld` format.

  ## 11. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  **Q: Are these original/branded shoes?**
  A: Yes, all Zen Store products are 100% authentic with quality assurance.

  **Q: Do you have a physical store?**
  A: We are an online-only retailer at this time.

  **Q: Do you offer gift wrapping?**
  A: Not currently, but we are considering it for the future.

  **Q: How do I know if an item is in stock?**
  A: All items shown on the website are in stock. If checkout fails for stock reasons, customer service will reach out within 24 hours.

  **Q: Can I cancel my order after placing it?**
  A: Yes, if the order has not yet been shipped. Email support@zenstore.example with your order ID as soon as possible.

  **Q: I received a wrong or damaged item — what now?**
  A: Email support@zenstore.example within 7 days of delivery with photos and your order ID. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund.

  **Q: Do prices include tax?**
  A: Listed prices do not include tax. A 5% tax is added at checkout, separately from shipping.

  **Q: Do you have student/military discounts?**
  A: Not at this time. Watch the homepage for seasonal sales — items marked **Sale** show a discounted price.

  **Q: Where can I follow you on social media?**
  A: We're on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and YouTube — links are in the website footer.

  **Q: How do I track my order?**
  A: Email support@zenstore.example with your order ID (`ZEN-XXXXXX`) and we will share the latest status.

  **Q: Is my payment information secure?**
  A: Yes. EasyPaisa transactions are processed directly through their secure platform. We never store payment credentials. COD requires no payment data at all.
